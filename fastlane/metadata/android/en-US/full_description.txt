<p><i>Shitter</i> is a lightweight Android client for Twitter and Mastodon.</p>
<p><br><b>Features:</b></p>
<ul>
    <li>access to statuses, users and trends</li>
    <li>real timeline (chronological order)</li>
    <li>customizable theme</li>
    <li>media, link and poll viewer</li>
    <li>multi account support</li>
    <li>no ads, no app tracking, proxy support</li>
</ul>
